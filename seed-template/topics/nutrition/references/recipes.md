# Recipes

The person's growing recipe collection. Every entry has been checked against `dietary-profile`
at the time it was added — re-check when restrictions change. Record verdicts after the person
tries a recipe (tier 1) so suggestions get better over time.

Rules for suggesting recipes:

- Check `dietary-profile` first, every time — including "obviously safe" dishes (hidden
  ingredients, cross-contamination).
- Match the person's skill, time budget, household size, and equipment (see the nutrition
  section in `main`).
- Prefer variations of loved recipes over cold starts; introduce at most [1–2] genuinely new
  recipes per week.
- When adapting a classic recipe around a restriction, say what was substituted and why.

Entry format:

```
## [Recipe name] — [tags: meal type, cuisine, effort ≤30min/60min/project]
**Restriction check:** [safe because … / substitutions made]
**Serves / keeps:** [portions; leftover & freezing notes]
**Recipe:** [ingredients + short steps, or source]
**Verdict:** [date — loved / fine / never again; what to change next time]
```

(Starts empty — grows with every meal plan and every verdict.)

## Fallback list

[3–5 verified-safe, low-effort meals for exhausted days — filled during the first weeks.]
